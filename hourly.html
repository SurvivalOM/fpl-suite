    How the two modes work & phone tips

    Every hour from when I start: press Start and it chimes after each interval (e.g. every 60 min) until you Stop. Good for focused work sessions.

    On the clock · waking hours: chimes automatically at the top of each hour (3:00, 4:00, 5:00…) only inside your waking window, and is silent overnight. Press Start once to arm it; it keeps going day after day as long as the tab stays open.

    Pause (either mode): for a meeting or quiet time, pause for 15 min / 1 hour / etc. or until you resume — the chimes in that window are skipped. Stop turns it off completely.

    On your phone: open this file in your browser → menu → Add to Home Screen. Tap once to unlock sound. If the screen turns off the browser may pause timers — turn on Keep screen awake for the most reliable ringing.

    Starts with Windows & auto-runs: the "Open Chant Reminder" launcher and the Startup shortcut open this in its own window and arm it automatically. To stop it opening at login, press Win+R, type shell:startup, and delete the Chant Reminder shortcut there.

    Your settings & log are saved on this device automatically.
